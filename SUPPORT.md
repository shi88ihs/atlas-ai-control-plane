# Support

## Community Support
We welcome questions, discussions, and ideas from the community.
- **GitHub Discussions:** Use the Discussions tab for general questions, feature requests, or sharing how you use Atlas.

## GitHub Issues
If you encounter a reproducible bug or have a specific feature request, please open a GitHub Issue.
- Include output from `control-plane doctor` (scrubbed of any secrets).
- Provide reproduction steps.

## Commercial Support
*(Planned)* Dedicated commercial support and private onboarding consultations are planned for enterprise teams moving AI fleets into production. Details will be announced alongside the `v1.0.0` release.
